# Bone & Monster Parts

> **Used by:** Smithing, Alchemy, Enchanting (cross-discipline)
> **Strengths:** Exotic special properties inherited from source creature
> **Best for:** Unique equipment, monster-themed builds, specialized resistances

Back to [Ingredients Index](index.md)

---

## Monster Part Categories

| Part Type | Source Examples | Used In | Properties |
|-----------|---------------|---------|------------|
| **Bones** | Troll bones, giant bones, dragon bones | Smithing (weapons, armor) | Structural strength, creature echoes |
| **Fangs/Claws** | Wolf fangs, bear claws, spider mandibles | Smithing (weapon components) | Natural weapon properties (poison, rend) |
| **Organs** | Troll heart, wyvern gizzard, harpy vocal cords | Alchemy (potent reagents) | Concentrated creature essences |
| **Ichor/Blood** | Spider ichor, basilisk blood, wyrm blood | Alchemy, Enchanting | Magical catalysts, venoms |
| **Shells/Carapace** | Giant beetle shell, cave crab armor | Smithing (armor plating) | Natural armor, lightweight protection |
| **Eyes/Gems** | Cyclops eye, wraith essence, phoenix ash | Enchanting (power sources) | Magical foci, enchantment amplifiers |
