# Cloth & Weave

> **Used by:** Enchanting, Alchemy (robes, wraps)
> **Strengths:** Magic channeling, enchantment capacity, spell cost reduction
> **Best for:** Robes, enchanting bases, magical garments

Back to [Ingredients Index](index.md)

---

## Fabric Types

| Fabric | Properties | Source | Specialty |
|--------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **Linen** | Breathable, common | Flax plants, merchants | Basic robes, bandages |
| **Silkweave** | Smooth, magic-receptive | Silkmoths, weavers' guilds | Enchantment base, comfortable wear |
| **Moonthread** | Luminous, mana-conductive | Moonbloom flowers, night harvest | Mana regen, spell channeling |
| **Voidcloth** | Dark, spell-absorbing | Planar tears, abyssal spiders | Spell resistance, ward capacity |
| **Aetherweave** | Shimmering, reality-thin | Arcane convergences, ley lines | Maximum enchantment capacity |

---

## Weaving

Cloth is woven from raw fiber at a loom (found in weavers' shops, player homes). Quality depends on Enchanting skill:

| Fiber Quality | Resulting Fabric Tier |
|---------------|----------------------|
| Common Fiber | Crude–Standard |
| Treated Fiber | Standard–Fine |
| Enchanted Fiber | Fine–Pure |
| Ley-Touched Fiber | Pure–Pristine |
