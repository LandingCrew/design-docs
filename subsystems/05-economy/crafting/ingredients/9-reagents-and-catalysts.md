## Elemental Essences

| Essence | Element | Source | Used For |
|---------|---------|--------|----------|
| **Cinder Essence** | Fire | Fire creatures, volcanic vents | Fire potions, fire enchantments, fire plating |
| **Hoarfrost Essence** | Frost/Cold | Ice creatures, frozen peaks | Frost potions, frost enchantments, frost plating |
| **Voltstone Shard** | Lightning/Shock | Storm creatures, exposed ridges | Shock potions, shock enchantments, shock plating |
| **Miasma Droplet** | Poison/Acid | Venomous creatures, swamps | Poison potions, corrosive enchantments |
| **Luminite Fragment** | Radiant/Holy | Sacred sites, divine creatures | Healing potions, radiant enchantments, radiant plating |
| **Nullshard** | Arcane/Magic | Magic anomalies, enchanted ruins | Dispelling, arcane plating, anti-magic effects |

## Catalysts

| Catalyst | Effect | Source |
|----------|--------|--------|
| **Binder Powder** | Stabilizes multi-ingredient potions | Ground minerals, alchemy shops |
| **Quickite** | Reduces crafting time | Mercury-like liquid metal, rare mines |
| **Resonance Crystal** | Amplifies enchantment power | Crystal caves, enchanted veins |
| **Essence of Binding** | Allows modifier stacking | Distilled from multiple essences |
| **Purity Salt** | Increases effective material tier by 1 | Evaporated from magical springs |
