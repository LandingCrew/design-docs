# Wood

> **Used by:** Smithing (bows, staves, shields), Enchanting (staves, wands)
> **Strengths:** Ranged weapons, magical conduits, lightweight shields
> **Best for:** Bows, crossbows, staves, wands, wooden shields

Back to [Ingredients Index](index.md)

---

## Wood Types

| Wood | Properties | Source | Specialty |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **Ashwood** | Flexible, strong | Common forests | General-purpose bows, staves |
| **Ironbark** | Dense, nearly metal-hard | Old-growth forests | Heavy shields, crossbow limbs |
| **Whisperwillow** | Light, resonant | Swamp willows, river groves | Stealth bows, sound-dampened |
| **Emberpine** | Resinous, fire-resistant | Volcanic forests, fire-touched groves | Fire-resistant equipment, torches |
| **Spiritwood** | Pale, magic-channeling | Sacred groves, ley-line forests | Staves, wands, magical conduits |
| **Elderwood** | Ancient, nearly petrified | Ancient forest hearts, druid sanctuaries | Legendary bows/staves, immense durability |

---

## Harvesting & Treatment

| Stage | Method | Result |
|-------|--------|--------|
| **Green Wood** | Freshly cut | Workable but warps; crude purity |
| **Seasoned** | Dried/aged | Stable, standard purity |
| **Shaped** | Steam-bent, carved | Optimized form, fine purity |
| **Laminated** | Layered, glued, compressed | Enhanced strength, pure purity |
| **Heartwood** | Core of ancient trees only | Pristine purity, legendary quality |
