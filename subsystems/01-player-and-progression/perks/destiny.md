# Destiny

**Destiny** (Req: 1)
- Increases carry weight by 25.

**Focus** (Req: 5)
- Your magic Regeneration is increased by 50%

**Constitution** (Req: 5)
- Your Health Regeneration is increased by 50%

**Endurance** (Req: 5)
- Your Stamina Regeneration is increased by 50%

**Mage** (Req: 10)
- Your spells and enchantments cost 5% less magic

**Warrior** (Req: 10)
- You deal 5% more weapon damage

**Thief** (Req: 10)
- You are 10% harder to detect

**Fortification** (Req: 15)
- Your Health, magic and Stamina are increased by 10

**magic Surge** (Req: 15)
- Your magic Regeneration is increased by 400% when your magic falls below 25%

**magic Shell** (Req: 15)
- Your magic resistance is increased by 10%

**Iron Skin** (Req: 15)
- Every 10 seconds, taking damage increases your Armor Rating by 150 for 6 seconds

**Battle Spirit** (Req: 15)
- Power attacks increase your attack damage by 10% for 6 seconds

**Adrenaline** (Req: 15)
- Your Stamina Regeneration is increased by 200% when your Stamina falls below 25%

**Swiftness** (Req: 15)
- Every 10 seconds, taking damage increases your movement speed by 20% for 6 seconds

**Agent** (Req: 20)
- Enemy attacks against you slows them by 25% for 5 seconds

**Sorcerer** (Req: 20)
- You take 10% less damage while casting spells

**Spellsword** (Req: 20)
- You restore 15 magic per weapon attack

**Knight** (Req: 20)
- You take 10% less damage while power attacking, drawing a bow, or blocking

**Endurance** (Req: 20)
- You regenerate stamina 100% faster

**Scout** (Req: 20)
- Your bows, crossbows and throwing knives do 10% more damage.

**Spellblade** (Req: 20)
- Attacks with weapons have a 30% chance to interrupt casting enemies

**Leadership** (Req: 25)
- Nearby allies move 15% faster during combat

**Hard Skin** (Req: 25)
- Your Armor Rating is increased by 100

**Healing Presence** (Req: 25)
- You heal yourself and nearby allies for 10 Health every 10 seconds

**Absorption** (Req: 25)
- You have a 10% chance to absorb the magic from incoming spells

**Conjuring Presence** (Req: 25)
- Nearby summoned or reanimated allies have 50 increased Health,, 50 increases Armor Rating and reflect 15% physical damage

**Corrosive Weapon** (Req: 25)
- Power attacks reduces enemies magic resistance by 15% and Armor Rating by 75 for 10 seconds

**Leeching Strikes** (Req: 25)
- You absorb 15 Health over 3 seconds from enemies attacking you once every 10 seconds for each enemy. While below 25% Health, absorbed Health is doubled

**Unyielding** (Req: 25)
- While blocking, your health, stamina and magic regeneration are increased by 25%

**Overwhelming Presence** (Req: 25)
- Close enemies have 100 reduced Armor Rating

**Smash** (Req: 25)
- Power attacks deal 10% more damage

**Trickster** (Req: 25)
- You have a 10% chance to evade enemy weapon attacks

**Swift Shadow** (Req: 25)
- You move 20% faster while sneaking

**Viper's Blade** (Req: 25)
- Power attacks reduces enemies poison resistance by 50% for 10 seconds

**Evasion** (Req: 25)
- You have a 15% chance to evade spells

**Silent Blade** (Req: 25)
- Your attacks deal 25 damage to magic

**Bard** (Req: 30)
- Nearby allies deal 15% more physical damage and have 15% magic resistance during combat

**Shaman** (Req: 30)
- You deal 20% more damage against impaired enemies

**Templar** (Req: 30)
- Every 90 seconds, falling below 10% Health, you are immune to any damage for 6 seconds and heal yourself afterwards for 100 Health

**Archmage** (Req: 30)
- Your Spells are 15% stronger or last 30% longer

**Summoner** (Req: 30)
- You can summon or reanimate one additional minion

**Battlemage** (Req: 30)
- Power attacks cost 25% less stamina if you have a spell in your left hand. Spell & enchantment cost 10% less if you have a weapon in your right hand

**Blood Knight** (Req: 30)
- While above 50% Health you deal 10% more weapon damage and while below 50% Health, you take 25% less magic damage

**General** (Req: 30)
- Your weapon damage and Armor Rating is increased by 10%

**Monk** (Req: 30)
- Your attacks deal 50 bleeding damage over 5 seconds

**Berserker** (Req: 30)
- You take 25% less damage while power attacking and power attack stamina usage is reduced by 20%

**Saboteur** (Req: 30)
- Your attacks have a 15% chance to stagger enemies. You deal 25% more damage against staggered enemies

**Assassin** (Req: 30)
- Every 10 seconds, your next attack deals 100% more damage

**Rogue** (Req: 30)
- Your attacks deal 50 poison damage over 10 seconds

**Stalker** (Req: 30)
- Your attacks slows enemies by 50% for 3 seconds

**Nightblade** (Req: 30)
- Against enemies that are out of magic, your attacks deal 20% more damage and have a 30% chance to dispel magic
