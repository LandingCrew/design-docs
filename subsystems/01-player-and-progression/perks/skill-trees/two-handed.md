# Two-Handed

**Great Weapon Mastery** (Req: 0)
- Your improved fighting techniques increase your damage dealt with two-handed weapons. (20% more damage)

**Great Weapon Mastery** (Req: 20)
- Your improved fighting techniques increase your damage dealt with two-handed weapons even more. (40% more damage)

**Barbaric Might** (Req: 20)
- Your power attacks become mighty blows that will penetrate armor and are less tiring. (-30% power attack stamina cost, +30% armor penetration)

**Quarterstaff Focus** (Req: 25)
- You've learned the basics of quarterstaff combat. (Deal 10 stamina damage on-hit, +5% critical hit chance)

**Quarterstaff Focus** (Req: 50)
- You've acquired the advanced techniques of quarterstaff combat. (Deal 15 stamina damage on-hit, +10% critical hit chance)

**Quarterstaff Focus** (Req: 75)
- You've become a master of quarterstaff combat. (Deal 20 stamina damage on-hit, +15% critical hit chance)

**Defensive Strike** (Req: 50)
- You can more efficiently block and parry incoming attacks with quarterstaves. (quarterstaves block 50% better, and bashes do twice the damage)

**Hafted Blade Focus** (Req: 25)
- You've become familiar with battle axes and halberds. (Attacks deal 40 extra damage over 20 seconds, +10% armor penetration)

**Hafted Blade Focus** (Req: 50)
-  become an advanced battle axe and halberd fighter. (Attacks deal 60 extra damage over 20 seconds, +20% armor penetration)

**Hafted Blade Focus** (Req: 75)
- You've become a master of battle axe and halberd combat. (Attacks deal 80 extra damage over 20 seconds, +30% armor penetration)

**Shield Breaker** (Req: 50)
- Your mighty swings can cleave through target's defense, hindering them from blocking next attacks. (power attacks reduce target's blockable damage by 90% and stamina regeneration by 25% for 10 seconds)

**Longblade Focus** (Req: 25)
- You've acquired the advanced techniques of using greatswords and pikes. (Attacks reduce target's melee damage by 10% for 10 seconds, +10% critical hit chance)

**Longblade Focus** (Req: 50)
- You've acquired the advanced techniques of using greatswords and pikes. (Attacks reduce target's melee damage by 15% for 10 seconds, +20% critical hit chance)

**Longblade Focus** (Req: 75)
- You've become a master of using greatswords and pikes. (Attacks reduce target's melee damage by 20% for 10 seconds, +30% critical hit chance)

**Reversal** (Req: 50)
- With enough momentum, you can slash deeper into targets with power attacks. (+25% critical hit chance and 2x critical hit damage when power attacking)

**Warhammer Focus** (Req: 25)
- You've learned some techniques to crush armor with warhammers. (+15% armor penetration, +5% critical hit chance)

**Warhammer Focus** (Req: 25)
- You've learned some techniques to crush armor with warhammers. (+30% armor penetration, +10% critical hit chance)

**Warhammer Focus** (Req: 25)
- You've learned some techniques to crush armor with warhammers. (+45% armor penetration, +15% critical hit chance)

**Onslaught** (Req: 50)
- You can deliver maximum force with your warhammer to knock off targets that fail to resist. (power attacks with warhammers knock off targets by 10% chance, 25% more damage against staggered targets.)

**Devastating Charge** (Req: 25)
- You've learned to perform a leaping power attack while sprinting, reducing your enemies to smithereens. (Able to do sprinting power attack which has a 50% chance to critical hit)

**Devastating Strike** (Req: 50)
- Your two-handed combat style has improved so much that all power attacks have become devastating. (25% more power attack damage, +10% critical hit chance)

**Hyper Armor** (Req: 75)
- Your power attacks are almost unstoppable. (Take 75% less poise damage while power attacking with two-handed weapons.)

**Mighty Strike** (Req: 100)
- You can fell almost any foe with a single strike of your two-hander. (50% more poise damage)

**Warbringer** (Req: 100)
- Your barbaric instincts strengthen as you or your foe meet their end. (Your power attacks deal up to 100% more damage based on the difference between your health and your target's health)

**Battle Hide** (Req: 80)
- You take half damage from enemy attacks while power attacking or blocking with quarterstaves and battlestaves.

**Execute** (Req: 80)
- Standing power attacks with battleaxes and halberds execute targets below 25% Health, delivering a critical strike that deals twenty times critical damage.

**Critical Slam** (Req: 80)
- Whenever a standing power attack with a warhammer inflicts a critical strike, it deals 3% more critical damage per point of Stamina.

**Death or Glory** (Req: 80)
- Two-handed power attacks with greatswords and pikes deal up to 100% more damage below half Health. The damage bonus increases as your Health decreases.
