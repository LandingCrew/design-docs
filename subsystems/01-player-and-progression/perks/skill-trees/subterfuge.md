# Subterfuge

**Light Fingers** (Req: 0)
- Your nimble fingers can lift small items from pockets undetected. Pickpocketing success chance increased by 25%.

**Master Thief** (Req: 50)
- Your hands move like shadows. You can now pickpocket equipped weapons and jewelry, and your pickpocketing success chance is increased by an additional 25%.

**Perfect Touch** (Req: 100)
- There is nothing you cannot take. Pickpocket equipped armor, enchanted items, and even quest items. Pickpocketing is 50% easier.

**Fencing** (Req: 25)
- You've established connections with the criminal underworld. Fences will buy your stolen goods at better prices (25% markup).

**Black Market** (Req: 75)
- Your reputation in the criminal world opens doors. All merchants will buy stolen goods (at reduced prices), and fences buy at near-market value (75% of normal price).

**Masquerade** (Req: 50)
- You've become proficient at acting, and with the right equipment you can now disguise yourself as a Forsworn, Imperial, Stormcloak, Necromancer, Thalmor or Warlock to (usually) avoid detection.

**Master of Disguise** (Req: 100)
- There is no role you cannot play, and your acting is so believable that there is almost no way for others to see through your disguises. You can even impersonate specific individuals.

**Streetwise** (Req: 25)
- You know the backalleys and criminal networks of every city. Fast travel to city gates even when guards are hostile, and gain 25% better prices from fences.

**Shadow Contacts** (Req: 50)
- Your criminal network provides useful intel. Discover quest shortcuts, learn about valuable targets, and occasionally receive tips about unmarked treasures.

**Chronology** (Req: 5)
- You've attuned yourself to temporal forces, granting you limited control over time flow. Time power cooldown is reduced by 10%.

**Temporal Potency** (Req: 25)
- Your mastery over time manipulation deepens, allowing you to affect larger areas and more powerful foes. Time powers have 15% greater magnitude and affect enemies up to 5 levels higher.

**Unbound by Time** (Req: 50)
- Your connection to temporal forces protects you from similar effects. Enemy time manipulation and slow effects are reduced by 50%.

**Extended Moment** (Req: 50)
- You've learned to stretch the fabric of time further. Your time dilation effects last 25% longer.

**Temporal Mastery** (Req: 75)
- Your understanding of time has reached new heights. Unlock a power that allows more frequent use of time abilities. (Grants a daily power that resets all time power cooldowns)

**Slow Time** (Req: 0)
- Project a sphere of slowed time around a target area, making all enemies within move and attack at 50% speed for 10 seconds. (60 second cooldown)

**Time Freeze** (Req: 25)
- Lock a single enemy completely in time, rendering them immobile and invulnerable for 8 seconds. Effect breaks if target takes damage. (90 second cooldown)

**Bullet Time** (Req: 50)
- Bend time around yourself, moving at normal speed while everything else slows to 30%. Lasts 6 seconds. (120 second cooldown)

**Jaunt** (Req: 50)
- Channel temporal energy to move in short bursts of accelerated time, gaining 50% movement and attack speed for 5 seconds. (45 second cooldown)

**Time Focus I** (Req: 0)
- Your mastery of temporal manipulation grows stronger with experience. (Time powers are 5% more powerful, cooldown reduced by 10%) [Requires Level 10]

**Time Focus II** (Req: 0)
- Your mastery of temporal manipulation grows stronger with experience. (Time powers are 10% more powerful, cooldown reduced by 20%) [Requires Level 20]

**Time Focus III** (Req: 0)
- Your mastery of temporal manipulation grows stronger with experience. (Time powers are 15% more powerful, cooldown reduced by 30%) [Requires Level 30]
