# Marksman

**Ranged Combat Training** (Req: 0)
- After some training at the shooting range you can deal more damage with ranged weapons. (20% more damage, ammunition grants armor penetration)

**Ranger** (Req: 15)
- You improved your footwork when engaging in ranged combat with light weapons. (Able to run with a drawn light bow or crossbow)

**Eagle Eye** (Req: 25)
- By concentrating only on your target and holding your breath, you can now aim much more precisely. (Zoom in by holding right mouse button)

**Marksman's Focus** (Req: 50)
- When fully focusing on your target, everything around you seems to slow down remarkably. (Time slows down while zooming, +10% critical hit chance)

**Rapid Reload** (Req: 50)
- Your have familiarized yourself with the mechanics of your crossbow. (+50% reload speed with heavy crossbows, +100% with light crossbows)

**Quick Shot** (Req: 50)
- You have learned to draw and fire in one single movement. (+50% draw speed with heavy bows, +100% with light bows)

**Power Shot** (Req: 75)
- You have discovered the art of aiming at tendons and joints. (Bows do 100% more poise damage, move forward and hold bash to perform power shot when over 200 stamina)

**Precise Aim** (Req: 20)
- Your aim is now precise enough to target weak spots in the enemy's defenses. (20% more damage)

**Piercing Shot** (Req: 40)
- You've learned how to hit the weakest parts of armor. (+50% armor penetration from ammunition)

**Penetrating Shot** (Req: 80)
- You've mastered the technique of hitting weak armor parts. (+50% armor penetration from ammunition)

**Stunning Precision** (Req: 100)
- Any successful staggering shot will almost always stun the target momentarily. (Staggering hits will also stun the target)

**Sniper** (Req: 15)
- You can keep steady aim with heavy ranged weapons, dealing more damage with sneak attacks. (1.5x irresistible sneak attack damage with heavy bow or crossbow)

**Bow Strike** (Req: 25)
- You can use your bow or crossbow as a melee weapon in close combat. (Double damage with bow bash, bow bash staggers targets, 50% less bow bash stamina cost)

**Knife Expertise** (Req: 15)
- Throwing knives penetrate the flesh, interrupting the target. (25% chance to stagger targets with throwing knives)

**Hunter** (Req: 0)
- You are more adapted to the wildlife, and better understand its inhabitants. (Do 20% more damage to animals and take 10% less damage from animals, +10% poison and disease resistance) [Requires Level 10]

**Hunter** (Req: 0)
- You are more adapted to the wildlife, and better understand its inhabitants. (Do 40% more damage to animals and take 20% less damage from animals, +20% poison and disease resistance) (Requires Level 20)

**Sharp Throw** (Req: 50)
- You can throw knives more precisely. (50% more damage with throwing knives)

**Focus on the Prey** (Req: 30)
- Cannot be staggered while holding a drawn bow or reloading a crossbow.

**Three Crows** (Req: 75)
- Shooting a target beyond 25 feet three times in rapid succession, leaving less than 2.75 seconds between each hit, deals bonus damage equal to 15% of the target's current Health (max. 150 damage) and knocks the target to the ground.

**Lion's Arrow** (Req: 100)
- Grants the 'Lion's Arrow' power: use it to store the spell you are dual casting. Shooting a fully drawn bow in combat also releases the stored spell in the direction of the crosshair. Only works with spells that affect other targets.
