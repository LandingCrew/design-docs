# Sneak

**Stealth** (Req: 0)
- You know the basics of moving silently and are 30% harder to detect when sneaking while not wearing any heavy armor.

**Stealth** (Req: 20)
- You are 60% harder to detect when sneaking while not wearing any heavy armor.

**Deft Strike** (Req: 25)
- You've learned to exploit weaknesses in your foes' armor when sneak attacking with daggers, bows or swords.

**Anatomical Lore** (Req: 50)
- Newfound knowledge of humanoid anatomy allows you to slay almost any human-like creature outright with sneak attacks. (5x/2x sneak attack damage with melee/ranged weapons on humanoids, 2x/1.5x on targets immune to sneak attacks)

**Advanced Anatomical Lore** (Req: 75)
- Your vast knowledge of anatomy allows you to perform devastating sneak attacks on almost any target. (5x/2x sneak attack damage with melee/ranged weapons on all targets, 3x/2x on targets immune to sneak attacks)

**Muffled Movement** (Req: 25)
- Your newfound finesse allows you to move more quietly while not wearing any heavy armor.

**Light Steps** (Req: 50)
- By distributing your weight more consciously, you're able to move more freely while sneaking and avoid triggering pressure plates or similar mechanisms.

**Acrobatics** (Req: 75)
- Your improved balance allows you to silently roll forward, avoid getting knocked down except through magic and move even more quietly.

**Shadowrunner** (Req: 100)
- There's almost nothing you cannot evade. You are even harder to detect, more silent and all falling damage is decreased.

**Arcane Assassin** (Req: 25)
- You can suppress the noise cast by your spell and deliver stronger effect to unwary targets. (Spells and enchantments are silent when cast out of combat, spells are 10% more powerful on targets while undetected)

**Arcane Assassin** (Req: 50)
- You can suppress the noise cast by your spell and deliver stronger effect to unwary targets. (Spells and enchantments are silent when cast out of combat, spells are 30% more powerful on targets while undetected)

**Arcane Assassin** (Req: 75)
- You can suppress the noise cast by your spell and deliver stronger effect to unwary targets. (Spells and enchantments are silent when cast out of combat, spells are 50% more powerful on targets while undetected)

**Problem Solver** (Req: 80)
- Sneak attacks deal 10% more damage for each 200 points of Health the target has, up to 50% more damage.

**Fog of War** (Req: 40)
- Sneaking is 15% more effective against targets that are in combat with you, or 30% if they are in combat with someone else.

**Tripwire** (Req: 20)
- Grants the 'Tripwire' power. At will, places a tripwire in front of you for 120 seconds. It snaps when tripped, knocking all targets hit by it to the floor.

**Whiplash** (Req: 50)
- Tripwire also reduces armor rating by 250 points for 10 seconds.

**Cloak and Dagger** (Req: 70)
- Breaking invisibility with a power attack is a guaranteed critical strike that deals 50% more critical damage.
