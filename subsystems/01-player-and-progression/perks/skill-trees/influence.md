# Influence

**Haggling** (Req: 0)
- You've learned how to deal with merchants effectively. Prices are 10% better and improve by 1% per skill level.

**Silver Tongue** (Req: 25)
- You've spent time improving your rhetorical skills. Persuasion and intimidation checks are much more likely to succeed, and you gain 15% better prices from the opposite gender.

**Merchant** (Req: 50)
- You can invest gold to permanently increase the amount of gold that merchants have. Merchants will buy any non-stolen item, and your sell price is increased by 15%.

**Master Trader** (Req: 100)
- Your reputation as a fair dealer precedes you. All merchants have 50% more gold, and you can sell items for 25% more than their base value.

**Intimidating Presence** (Req: 25)
- Your physical or social authority makes others think twice. Intimidation attempts are 50% more effective, and weaker enemies may flee before combat starts.

**War Cry** (Req: 50)
- Overwhelm your enemies with a roaring shout, striking fear into their hearts and sending weaker enemies fleeing. (Living enemies up to level 30 do 20% less damage, enemies up to level 20 flee from combat for one minute)

**Commander I** (Req: 25)
- Your knowledge in combat tactics allows you to effectively lead your allies in combat. (Improve nearby allies' skills by 10%, increase magic, stamina, and their regeneration by 50%, increase unarmed damage by 5)

**Commander II** (Req: 50)
- Your knowledge in combat tactics allows you to effectively lead your allies in combat. (Improve nearby allies' skills by 20%, increase magic, stamina, and their regeneration by 100%, increase unarmed damage by 10)

**Commander III** (Req: 75)
- Your knowledge in combat tactics allows you to effectively lead your allies in combat. (Improve nearby allies' skills by 30%, increase magic, stamina, and their regeneration by 150%, increase unarmed damage by 15)

**Leadership** (Req: 75)
- Your rhetorical skill makes you the perfect leader. By inspiring all those who fight by your side, followers gain 20% damage, -15% spell cost, +75% block, +100 magic and stamina, 50 carry weight.

**Rally** (Req: 50)
- Once per day, you can rally all nearby allies, granting them temporary courage. (Allies within 30 feet gain 50% damage resistance and 25% attack damage for 60 seconds)

**Battle Muse** (Req: 0)
- Your mastery of musical instruments turns the tide of battle. Drums inspire your allies, the flute soothes your foes and the lute stirs chaos among your enemies.

**Standing Ovation** (Req: 25)
- Your captivating performances leave the crowd in awe. The bonuses received from playing for a crowd at the inns are now doubled and last twice as long.

**Boozy Bard** (Req: 25)
- Alcohol makes the music flow sweeter! Alcoholic drinks no longer blur your vision and performing under the influence enhances your musical powers, increasing their effectiveness.

**Windborne Melody** (Req: 25)
- Your music flows as freely as the wind. Playing an instrument no longer slows your movement.

**Skald** (Req: 50)
- Continuously playing instruments during combat now grants additional benefits. Drums bolster your allies, while the Lute and Flute weaken your enemies' defenses.

**Symphony of Storms** (Req: 75)
- The bard's mastery over their instrument echoes through the heavens. The deep and powerful resonance of your music calls forth elemental storms, with each instrument summoning its own fury.
