# Heavy Armor

**Conditioning** (Req: 0)
- You've accustomed yourself to using heavy armor. (No stamina drain for wearing heavy armor, unique bonus when wearing high-quality materials, -35% armor weight penalty)

**Relentless Onslaught** (Req: 20)
- After extensive training you now can sprint in heavy armor without problems. (no sprinting stamina cost penalty, take 80% less melee damage when bullrushing, -10% armor weight penalty)

**Devastating Tackle** (Req: 50)
- When in full heavy armor, you can cause devastating impact when bull-rushing. (Bull-rush while wearing 4 or more pieces of heavy armor does double damage and has higher chance to knockdown)

**Combat Casting** (Req: 25)
- You are able to keep concentrated enough in heavy armor. (No spell cost penalty for casting Novice and Apprentice spells in heavy armor)

**Combat Trance** (Req: 50)
- Your heavy armor distracts you even less. (No spell cost penalty for casting Adept spells in heavy armor)

**Combat Meditation** (Req: 75)
- You're accustomed to your heavy armor so much that you can now cast even sophisticated spells with relative ease. (No spell cost penalty for casting Expert spells in heavy armor)

**Battle Mage** (Req: 100)
- You have mastered spellcasting while wearing heavy armor. (No spell cost penalty for casting Master spells in heavy armor, 15% less spell cost when wearing all heavy armor: head, chest, hands, feet)

**Combat Training** (Req: 25)
- You are less hindered by your armor and can attack with ease. (No power attack stamina cost penalty, increased unarmed damage with heavy gauntlets, -10% armor weight penalty)

**Immovable** (Req: 50)
- When in full heavy armor, you can pose a defensive stance to reduce incoming impact. (Resist physical, elemental and poise damage by 33% while attacking, casting, walking or standing still wearing 4 or more pieces of heavy armor)

**Fortitude** (Req: 50)
- Your training made you stronger and you are now less burdened by the rigidity of your armor. (+40 stamina, +20 carry weight, -10% armor weight penalty)

**Power of the Combatant** (Req: 75)
- Taking hits gives you a rush of adrenaline. (Restore 15 stamina over 6 seconds after taking a hit, -10% armor weight penalty)

**Juggernaut** (Req: 100)
- You've reached perfection in the skill of wearing heavy armor and are now significantly more maneuverable in it. (15% less armor weight, 10% more armor rating, 50% less poise damage when wearing all heavy armor: head, chest, hands, feet)

**Mounted Combat** (Req: 0)
- You can deal more damage with melee weapons while mounted. (1.5x melee attack damage while on horseback) [Requires Level 10]

**Cushioned** (Req: 30)
- You take half damage from falling if wearing all Heavy Armor.

**Defiance** (Req: 50)
- You are trained to deflect incoming attacks while wearing all Heavy Armor. Whenever an enemy attacks you, you gain 15 points of armor rating for 10 seconds. This effect stacks.

**Never Kneel** (Req: 75)
- If wearing all Heavy Armor, take 30% less attack damage from power attacks.

**Reap the Whirlwind** (Req: 100)
- When struck by a power attack or power bash, your attacks deal 50% more damage to the attacker for 5 seconds if wearing all Heavy Armor.
