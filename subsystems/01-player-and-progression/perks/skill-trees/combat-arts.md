# Combat Arts

**Improved Blocking** (Req: 0)
- You strengthened your shield arm and trained to parry blows a little. (Block 25% more damage with weapons and shields, take 25% less elemental damage when blocking with a shield and 10% less with a weapon)

**Experienced Blocking** (Req: 20)
- With weapon or shield you can block almost any blow with rock-solid confidence. (Block 50% more damage, recover some stamina when blocking a hit)

**Torch Combat** (Req: 25)
- You can use your torch more effectively in combat, causing painful burns. (Power bash with a torch does 10 extra fire damage and staggers the target, targets set on fire take 5 extra fire damage from weapon attacks)

**Strong Grip** (Req: 15)
- You hold on to your shield like you would hold on to your life. (50% less shield weight, 25% more armor rating, block 40% more damage, arrows that hit the shield do no damage)

**Elemental Protection** (Req: 50)
- You've learned how to block damaging spells more efficiently. (Take 50% less fire, frost, and shock damage when blocking with a shield)

**Defensive Stance** (Req: 75)
- You have honed your defensive movements to perfection. (Blocking with a shield does not slow you down, 75% less shield weight, 50% more armor rating)

**Powerful Bashes** (Req: 25)
- By gathering your strength for a short while, you can perform a much more powerful bash. (Able to do a power bash)

**Shield Strike** (Req: 25)
- You can use your shield more offensively, using it as a deadly weapon. (50% more shield bash damage, double damage when not wearing heavy armor, 25% less shield bash stamina cost)

**Shield Strike** (Req: 50)
- You can use your shield more offensively, using it as a deadly weapon. (100% more shield bash damage, double damage when not wearing heavy armor, 50% less shield bash stamina cost)

**Overpowering Bashes** (Req: 50)
- When you put all of your power into your shield bashes, you will often overpower your foes. (Power bashing with a shield can knock down)

**Disarming Bash** (Req: 75)
- You've trained in the art of disarming foes mid-combat. (20% chance to disarm when power bashing using a shield or weapon)

**Unstoppable Charge** (Req: 100)
- When bull rushing with your shield raised, you are a tremendous force to deal with. (Sprinting with a shield raised knocks down most targets and reduces incoming damage by 50%)

**Shield's Best Friend** (Req: 30)
- Like the Imperial Legions of old, you understand the advantage of shortswords in combat. (20% more bash damage, 20% more block and 20% less poise damage when wielding a shortsword)

**Sound Body** (Req: 30)
- You have unburdened your body. You gain 100 armor rating when not wearing armor and gain 10% armor penetration and 10 unarmed damage when unarmored.

**Sound Body** (Req: 50)
- You have unburdened your body. You gain 200 armor rating when not wearing armor and gain 20% armor penetration and 20 unarmed damage when unarmored.

**Sound Mind** (Req: 60)
- You've unburdened your inner thoughts, gaining 10% resistance to magic and 50% greater health, stamina and magic regen when unarmored.

**Sound Spirit** (Req: 80)
- You have unburdened your spirit and now shouts are 10% stronger and last 10% longer also you are 25% more resistant to diseases and poisons when unarmored.
