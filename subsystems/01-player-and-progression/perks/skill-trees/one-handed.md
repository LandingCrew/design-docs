# One-Handed

**Weapon Mastery** (Req: 0)
- Your improved fighting techniques allow you to swing one-handed weapons or fists with less effort and deal more damage. (20% more damage)

**Weapon Mastery** (Req: 20)
- Your improved fighting techniques increase your damage dealt with one-handed weapons or fists greatly. (40% more damage)

**Weapons Master** (Req: 30)
- You have rejected mastering one specific weapon type. Instead, you have begun to master versatility across all one-handed weapons. (15% more damage with any one-handed weapon)

**Weapons Master** (Req: 60)
- Your versatility with all one-handed weapons has become your greatest strength. (30% more damage with any one-handed weapon)

**Masterly Strike** (Req: 75)
- Your power attacks with any one-handed weapon are now devastating enough to penetrate enemy armor and less exhausting. (-30% power attack stamina cost, +30% armor penetration with any one-handed weapon)

**Penetrating Strikes** (Req: 20)
- Your power attacks with one-handed weapons or fists are now devastating enough to penetrate enemy armor and less exhausting. (-30% power attack stamina cost, +30% armor penetration)

**Short Blade Focus** (Req: 25)
- You learned some dirty tricks for your dagger or tanto. (2x irresistible sneak attack damage, +20% damage from behind, +5% critical hit chance)

**Short Blade Focus** (Req: 50)
- A dagger or tanto in your hand turns into a deadly weapon. (3x irresistible sneak attack damage, +30% damage from behind, +10% critical hit chance)

**Short Blade Focus** (Req: 75)
- You have mastered the deadly art of daggers and tantos. (4x irresistible sneak attack damage, +40% damage from behind, +15% critical hit chance)

**Puncture** (Req: 50)
- You can aim for critical weak points, dealing even more damage. (2x critical damage and +25% critical hit chance)

**War Axe Focus** (Req: 25)
- You've become familiar with war axes. (Attacks deal 20 extra damage over 20 seconds, +10% armor penetration)

**War Axe Focus** (Req: 50)
- You've become an advanced war axe fighter. (Attacks deal 30 extra damage over 20 seconds, +20% armor penetration)

**War Axe Focus** (Req: 75)
- You've become a master of war axe combat. (Attacks deal 40 extra damage over 20 seconds, +30% armor penetration)

**Armsbreaker** (Req: 50)
- The blade of your war axe can carve into target's weapons and shields, weakening their damage and protection. (power attacks with war axes reduce target's blockable damage by 80% and stamina regeneration by 25% for 10 seconds)

**Mace Focus** (Req: 25)
- You've learned some techniques to crush armor with maces. (10% more power attack damage, +15% armor penetration)

**Mace Focus** (Req: 50)
- You've become an advanced mace fighter and know how to find weak spots in enemy armor. (20% more power attack damage, +30% armor penetration)

**Mace Focus** (Req: 75)
- You've become a masterly mace fighter, rendering armor almost useless. (30% more power attack damage, +45% armor penetration)

**Stunning Blow** (Req: 50)
- You can cause stunning impact to targets that fail to block, knocking them down to the ground. (unblocked power attacks with maces knock down targets by 10% chance, 25% more damage against staggered targets)

**Sword & Spear Focus** (Req: 25)
- You've learned the basics of using 1 handed swords, spears, javelins, rapiers and katanas. (Attacks reduce target's melee damage by 10% for 10 seconds, +10% critical hit chance)

**Sword & Spear Focus** (Req: 50)
- You've acquired the advanced techniques of using 1 handed swords and spears. (Attacks reduce target's melee damage by 15% for 10 seconds, +20% critical hit chance)

**Sword & Spear Focus** (Req: 75)
- You've become a master of using 1 handed swords and spears. (Attacks reduce target's melee damage by 20% for 10 seconds, +30% critical hit chance)

**Disarming Strike** (Req: 50)
- Your masterful strikes allow you to disarm enemies while attacking. (power attacks with swords, spears, javelins, rapiers and katanas disarm attacking targets by 15% chance)

**Powerful Charge** (Req: 25)
- You have learned to perform a leaping power attack while sprinting. (Able to do sprinting power attack which has a 50% critical hit chance)

**Powerful Strike** (Req: 50)
- You know how to put the maximum force into your power attacks. (20% more power attack damage, +10% critical hit chance)

**Relentless Onslaught** (Req: 100)
- While attacking a target, build up momentum for a decisive strike. (Each normal attack with one-handed weapons increases power attack damage on target by 10% for 5s. Stacks up to six times - three while dual-wielding)

**Flurry** (Req: 25)
- When fighting with dual or single one-handed weapons or fists, you are able to hit enemy weak points better. (+15% critical hit chance)

**Flurry** (Req: 50)
- When fighting with dual or single one-handed weapons or fists, you're able to hit enemy weak points even better. (+30% critical hit chance)

**Storm of Steel** (Req: 75)
- You've mastered the art of using multiple weapons in combat. (50% more dual-wield power attack poise damage with any weapon, unlocks secondary dagger abilities when using a bow)

**Dual Flurry** (Req: 30)
- Your dual-wielding technique has improved, allowing faster strikes. (20% faster attack speed when dual-wielding, -10% stamina cost)

**Dual Flurry** (Req: 60)
- Your dual-wielding mastery enables lightning-fast combinations. (35% faster attack speed when dual-wielding, -20% stamina cost)

**Dual Savagery** (Req: 50)
- Your dual-wield power attacks become devastating whirlwinds of steel. (50% more dual-wield power attack damage, power attacks with two weapons cost 25% less stamina)

**Twin Souls** (Req: 75)
- You can enchant both weapons simultaneously when dual-wielding. (Dual-wielded enchantments are 25% more effective, both weapons can have the same enchantment and it stacks)

**Hand to Hand** (Req: 0)
- You are trained in hand to hand combat, and can effectively disable opponents with bare hands. (+25 unarmed damage, unarmed attacks damage target stamina by half the unarmed damage)

**Grappling** (Req: 25)
- You can grapple opponents to disarm or immobilize them. (unarmed power attacks stagger targets by 50% chance and disarm by 10% chance)

**Takedown** (Req: 50)
- You can knock down enemies by grappling. (unarmed power attacks knock down targets by 10% chance)

**Unarmed Defense** (Req: 25)
- You can defend yourself better even without weapons or shields. (15% less physical damage taken for each hand empty)

**Unarmed Stance** (Req: 50)
- While unarmed, you can maneuver more freely and resist physical force. (+5% movement speed for each hand empty, +50 mass when both hands are empty)

**Armed Spelllcasting** (Req: 50)
- You can balance your weapon well with a single hand, and can use it to cast magic. (+25% poise damage and 10% less spell cost when left hand is empty or wielding magic)

**Balanced Wielding** (Req: 50)
- You can balance your weapon better when wielding it in a single hand, delivering quick and precise attacks. (+25% damage against enemies who are attacking and +15% armor penetration when left hand is empty)

**Final Stand** (Req: 80)
- Power attacks deal up to 100% more damage below half Health. The damage bonus increases as your Health decreases.

**Heavy Slam** (Req: 80)
- Whenever a standing power attack with a mace inflicts a critical strike, it deals 3% more critical damage per point of Stamina.

**Guillotine** (Req: 80)
- Standing power attacks with war axes execute targets below 25% Health, delivering a critical strike that deals twenty times critical damage.

**Death Adder** (Req: 80)
- Forward power attacks with a dagger ignore 25% of armor.
