## Magic Build Archetypes

### Pure Mage (Robes Only)

**Build Philosophy:**
Glass cannon - maximum magical power and spell variety at the cost of physical defense and extreme poise vulnerability.

**Stats Distribution:**
- **HP:** Low (150-250 at high level)
- **Memory Capacity:** Very High (80-100+ memory at high level)
- **SP:** Medium (200-250)
- **Education & Intuition:** Very High (60-80+ each for memory capacity)

**Armor:** Robes only (very low poise, maximum mobility, vulnerable to spell loss)

**Skills Invested:**
1. **3-4 Magic Schools** (70-100 in each)
   - Primary: Evocation (damage)
   - Secondary: Divine Arts (healing/wards)
   - Tertiary: Abjuration (armor spells) OR Spirit Calling (summons)
   - Optional: Illusion OR Mysticism
2. **Enchanting** (50-100) - Self-enchant gear
3. **Alchemy** (30-60) - magic potions

**Playstyle:**
- Stay at maximum range
- Open with summons or armor spells
- Devastating spell damage
- Heal/ward when threatened
- Retreat if enemies close distance

**Strengths:**
- Largest spell variety (can prepare 10-15+ spells)
- Mastered spells have very low failure rates (5-10%)
- Access to all master spells (multiple 7-memory spells prepared)
- Ultimate magical versatility
- Can adapt to any situation via large loadout
- Highest spell damage potential

**Weaknesses:**
- **Very low poise** - single hit can break poise
- **Catastrophic memory loss risk** - holding expensive spell (6-7 memory) when hit = massive loss
- Dies in 2-3 hits from melee
- Must play perfectly or lose critical spells
- Cannot channel safely (too vulnerable to poise break)
- Expensive (spell books, clarity potions)

**Memory Management Strategy:**
- Master 4-6 signature spells (reduce memory cost via practice)
- Prepare diverse loadout (damage, healing, utility, crowd control)
- **NEVER hold expensive spells in danger** (6-7 memory loss is devastating)
- Quick-cast only, minimize holding time
- Use wards or summons to prevent poise breaks
- Carry Grand Clarity potions for emergencies (1000g insurance)

**Spell Loadout Example (90 memory capacity):**
- **Mastered Core Spells (4 spells, 20 memory):**
  - Fireball (5 memory, mastered from 7) - AoE damage
  - Lightning Bolt (4 memory, mastered from 5) - single-target
  - Greater Ward (4 memory, mastered from 5) - defense
  - Fast Healing (3 memory, mastered from 4) - emergency heal
- **Utility & Situational (6 spells, 25 memory):**
  - Flame Atronach summon (6 memory) - tank
  - Invisibility (5 memory) - escape
  - Paralyze (5 memory) - CC
  - Detect Life (3 memory) - scouting
  - Muffle (3 memory) - stealth
  - Reload (1 memory) - flexibility
- **Ultimate Spells (2 spells, 14 memory):**
  - Apocalypse (7 memory) - boss killer
  - Mass Paralysis (7 memory) - crowd control
- **Remaining capacity:** 31 memory for additional spells/variety

### Battle Mage (Light Armor)

**Build Philosophy:**
Balanced hybrid - competent in both melee and magic, master of neither. Uses Reload spell for tactical flexibility.

**Stats Distribution:**
- **HP:** Medium (250-350)
- **Memory Capacity:** Medium (45-60 memory at high level)
- **SP:** Medium-High (250-300)
- **Education & Intuition:** Medium (40-50 each)

**Armor:** Light armor (medium poise, reasonable spell protection, good mobility)

**Skills Invested:**
1. **1-2 Magic Schools** (50-75 in each)
   - Primary: Evocation OR Divine Arts
   - Secondary: Abjuration OR Illusion
2. **One-Handed** (70-100) - Melee damage
3. **Evasion** (Light armor skill, 70-100)
4. **Destruction/Alteration** for enchantments

**Playstyle:**
- Sword in right hand, spell in left
- Open with spell, close to melee
- Use spells for advantage (slow enemy, buff self)
- Heal between fights
- Mobile, adaptive combat

**Strengths:**
- Versatile (melee + magic options)
- Good mobility (light armor)
- Self-sufficient (healing)
- Can adapt to situations
- Balanced offense and defense

**Weaknesses:**
- Not as powerful as pure mage or pure warrior
- Split skill investment (slower progression)
- Limited MP pool (can't spam big spells)
- Medium defense (not tanky, not evasive)

**Spell Loadout Example:**
- **Right Hand:** One-handed sword (physical damage)
- **Left Hand:** Firebolt (ranged opener)
- **Quickslot 1:** Fast Healing (between fights)
- **Quickslot 2:** Ice Spike (slow rushing enemies)
- **Pre-buff:** Stoneflesh (60 armor)

### Spellsword (Heavy Armor)

**Build Philosophy:**
Tank with magical support - primarily melee with limited utility magic.

**Stats Distribution:**
- **HP:** High (350-450)
- **MP:** Low (150-200)
- **SP:** High (300-350)

**Armor:** Heavy armor (-40% max MP, -50% MP regen, high defense)

**Skills Invested:**
1. **1 Magic School** (40-60) - Usually Divine Arts
2. **One-Handed OR Two-Handed** (90-100)
3. **Heavy Armor** (90-100)
4. **Block** (70-90)

**Playstyle:**
- Frontline tank
- Use magic only for healing or utility
- Can't sustain magical combat
- Occasional spell to supplement melee
- Very defensive, slow, methodical

**Strengths:**
- High survivability (heavy armor + healing)
- Strong melee damage
- Can frontline tank
- Self-healing (reduces potion dependency)
- Simple, straightforward

**Weaknesses:**
- Very limited MP pool (150-200 MP total)
- Can only cast a few spells before depleted
- Slow movement (heavy armor)
- No offensive magic (MP too low)
- One-dimensional combat

**Spell Loadout Example:**
- **Right Hand:** One-handed weapon
- **Left Hand:** Healing (concentration, 15 MP/sec)
- **Quickslot 1:** Fast Healing (50 HP, 50 MP)
- **Quickslot 2:** Cure Disease (utility)
- **Shield:** Heavy shield (block)

**MP Usage:**
- 150 MP total after armor penalty
- Fast Healing = 50 MP (can cast 3 times)
- OR Healing concentration = 10 seconds (150 HP total)
- That's it - MP pool exhausted

### Summoner (Flexible Armor)

**Build Philosophy:**
Pet class - summons do the fighting, you support and manage.

**Stats Distribution:**
- **HP:** Medium (200-300)
- **MP:** High (350-450)
- **SP:** Medium (200-250)

**Armor:** Robes (preferred) or Light armor (for defense)

**Skills Invested:**
1. **Spirit Calling** (90-100) - MAXED for summon duration
2. **Divine Arts** (50-75) - Healing, buffs
3. **Evocation OR Illusion** (30-60) - Support damage or control
4. **Enchanting** (50-100) - Self-sufficiency

**Playstyle:**
- Summon 2-3 minions (with Twin Souls perk)
- Let summons tank and deal damage
- Support with heals, buffs, or occasional spells
- Resummon when durations expire
- Stay safe behind your army

**Strengths:**
- Safest solo build (minions take aggro)
- Continuous damage from summons
- Can handle multiple enemies (summons split aggro)
- Soul trap for enchanting (economic benefit)
- Low-risk playstyle

**Weaknesses:**
- Weak if summons die quickly
- High MP cost to maintain summons
- Micro-management (tracking summon durations)
- Less direct damage than pure mage
- Summons can body-block you

**Spell Loadout Example:**
- **Left Hand:** Flame Atronach (tank summon)
- **Right Hand:** Storm Atronach (DPS summon)
- **Quickslot 1:** Dremora Lord (ultimate tank, master spell)
- **Quickslot 2:** Healing (keep summons alive if perk allows)
- **Quickslot 3:** Soul Trap (farm souls)

**Summon Management:**
- 2 summons active (max without perk)
- Flame Atronach: 5 min duration (re-cast every 5 min)
- Storm Atronach: 5 min duration
- Total MP cost: 200 MP every 5 minutes
- MP regen over 5 min: 780 MP (2.6 MP/sec × 300 sec)
- Sustainable

### Illusionist (Stealth Mage)

**Build Philosophy:**
Never detected - manipulate enemies, avoid combat entirely if desired.

**Stats Distribution:**
- **HP:** Low (150-250)
- **MP:** Medium-High (300-400)
- **SP:** Medium (200-250)

**Armor:** Robes or Light armor (silent movement)

**Skills Invested:**
1. **Illusion** (90-100) - Core mechanic
2. **Sneak** (90-100) - Synergy with invisibility
3. **Abjuration** (40-60) - Utility, detect life
4. **One-Handed OR Archery** (50-70) - Sneak attacks (optional)

**Playstyle:**
- Invisibility + Muffle = perfect stealth
- Frenzy groups → watch them kill each other
- Calm hostiles → sneak past
- Paralyze targets → backstab
- Pacifist option viable (no killing required)

**Strengths:**
- Never detected (perfect stealth)
- Can complete quests without killing
- Enemies kill each other (Frenzy/Mayhem)
- Ultimate control (fear, calm, frenzy)
- Unique playstyle

**Weaknesses:**
- Useless in direct combat
- Illusion spells have level caps (high-level enemies immune)
- Expensive MP costs (invisibility spam)
- Struggles vs constructs/undead (illusion-immune)
- Requires careful planning

**Spell Loadout Example:**
- **Left Hand:** Invisibility (escape/stealth)
- **Right Hand:** Frenzy (turn enemies on each other)
- **Quickslot 1:** Muffle (silent movement)
- **Quickslot 2:** Calm (pacify hostiles)
- **Quickslot 3:** Paralyze (lock down target)

**Playstyle Example:**
1. Cast Muffle before dungeon
2. Sneak to enemy camp
3. Cast Invisibility
4. Cast Mayhem (all enemies attack each other)
5. Remain invisible, watch chaos
6. Loot corpses when done

### Necromancer (Undead Summoner)

**Build Philosophy:**
Master of death - summon undead, raise corpses, drain life.

**Stats Distribution:**
- **HP:** Medium (200-300)
- **MP:** High (350-450)
- **SP:** Low (150-200)

**Armor:** Robes (dark/gothic aesthetic)

**Skills Invested:**
1. **Spirit Calling** (90-100) - Undead summons
2. **Mysticism** (70-90) - Soul manipulation
3. **Evocation** (30-50) - Frost damage (thematic)
4. **Alchemy** (50-70) - Poisons

**Playstyle:**
- Raise corpses from battlefield
- Summon skeleton/wraith army
- Soul trap enemies for power
- Frost damage (thematic with death)
- Feared by NPCs (reputation)

**Strengths:**
- Free summons (raise corpses, no MP cost after initial)
- Army scales with enemy count (more corpses = more minions)
- Soul farming for enchanting
- Thematic and unique
- Can solo with undead army

**Weaknesses:**
- Requires corpses (no corpses = limited summons)
- Undead weak to Divine Arts (enemy clerics counter you)
- Reputation penalties (necromancy is illegal in most regions)
- Limited to undead summons (no atronachs)

**Spell Loadout Example:**
- **Left Hand:** Summon Skeleton
- **Right Hand:** Raise Dead (reanimate corpse)
- **Quickslot 1:** Soul Trap
- **Quickslot 2:** Frostbite (thematic damage)
- **Quickslot 3:** Summon Wraith
